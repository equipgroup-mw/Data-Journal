Sector vertical · 01

Equip Agri

Daily prices, yield estimates, and market-access data for Malawi's staple crops and export commodities — built for the people who plan food security responses and the people who trade.

Request access →

Institutional license. Public archive remains free; daily price feed and API require a subscription. Scholarly access granted on request.

What we track

  • Maize — ADMARC, farmgate, wholesale
  • Rice — local and imported grades
  • Cassava — fresh and dried
  • Groundnuts — confectionery and oil
  • Soya beans and soya cake
  • Pigeon peas and macadamia
  • Tobacco — auction and contract
  • District-level yield estimates
  • Rainfall anomalies, soil moisture
  • Fertilizer retail (NPK, Urea, CAN)

Who it's for

  • NGOs planning food-security responses
  • Government early-warning units
  • Commodity traders and processors
  • Development partners and donors
  • Research institutions, universities
  • Commercial farmers and cooperatives

Access model

  • Public archive — free, with attribution
  • Daily price feed — institutional license
  • Custom data pulls — project-based
  • API access — by request, rate-limited
Editorial standards

Every chart on Equip carries a source citation and a last-updated date. Every story is fact-checked against primary sources. When we get something wrong, we publish the correction on a dedicated Corrections page with a timestamp — not as a silent edit.
